Pork Chop Recipe

Pan-Seared Pork Chop with Garlic and Thyme

This is a classic pork chop recipe perfect for special occasions.

Pork Chops or Pork Loins are the recommended cuts for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pork Chop or Pork Loin (about an inch thick)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat up the pan on high heat
  2. While waiting for pan to heat up, season both sides of the pork chop with salt and pepper
  3. Add olive oil and place pork chop on pan (cook on both sides for 5-6 minutes)
  4. In the final two minutes of cooking time, add a table spoon of butter to the pan with the garlic and thyme
  5. Remove pork chop from pan and let it rest for 5 minutes
  6. Once rested, plate it to your liking and enjoy!
