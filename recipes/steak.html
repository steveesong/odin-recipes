Steak Recipe

Pan-Seared Steak with Garlic and Thyme

This is a classic steak recipe perfect for special occasions.

Rib-eye, New York Strip, or T-Bone are the recommended cuts for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat up the pan on medium heat
  2. While waiting for pan to heat up, season both sides of the steak with salt and pepper
  3. Add olive oil and place steak on pan (cook on both sides for 4 minutes)
  4. In the final two minutes of cooking time, add a table spoon of butter to the pan with the garlic and thyme
  5. Remove steak from pan and let it rest for 5 minutes
  6. Once rested, plate it to your liking and enjoy!
