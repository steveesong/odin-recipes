Chicken Recipe

Pan-Seared Chicken with Garlic and Thyme

This is a classic chicken recipe perfect for special occasions.

Chicken Breast or Chicken Thigh are the recommended cuts for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh or Butterflied Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat up the pan on high heat
  2. While waiting for pan to heat up, season both sides of the chicken with salt and pepper
  3. Add olive oil and place chicken on pan (cook on both sides for 5-6 minutes)
  4. In the final two minutes of cooking time, add a table spoon of butter to the pan with the garlic and thyme
  5. Remove chicken from pan and let it rest for 5 minutes
  6. Once rested, plate it to your liking and enjoy!
